# Task Brief: Using AI To Proxy Communication

The team will write a literature review subsection titled **Using AI to Proxy
Communication** for a broader paper on AI-mediated collaboration.

The review should explain how AI systems stand in for, filter, summarize,
translate, rephrase, or coordinate communication between people. The final text
should distinguish proxy communication from ordinary individual writing
assistance.

## Deliverable

A concise literature review subsection with:

- a definition of AI proxy communication
- a small taxonomy of proxy mechanisms
- a discussion of communication breakdowns and risks
- citations or evidence notes linked to a lightweight literature matrix
- a short final integration pass so definitions, evidence, and claims align

- Noah: source search and evidence matrix
- Lin: synthesis and draft background
- Priya: section outline, integration, and final merge

## Constraints

- Claims in the draft must trace back to the source matrix.
- Definitions must stay consistent across notes and draft text.

  visible in OpenCollab.
